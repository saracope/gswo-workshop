Launching Your Website

We will be following these instructions when we are ready launch the website:

  1. Login to your Divshot account.
  2. Set up a new Divshot app via the divshot.io dashboard.
  3. Choose the development platform on Divshot and upload your files.
  4. When you're happy with your site, you can promote them up to staging and when you're ready for it to go out into the world, promote it up to production.
